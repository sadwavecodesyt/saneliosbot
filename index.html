Stock Market

Invest in different stocks which randomly change their price based on the buys and sells of that stock!

Usage:

/stockmarket

Role Shop

Create a Role Shop for your server! Customize the names and prices of the roles!

Usage:

/roleshop

Economy Commands

There are many different economy commands in this bot! Some Commands even have RNG aspects that give a random chance of earning more money!

Usage:

/economy

Moderation Commands

There are many moderation features! Auto-moderation is hard coded into the bot to automatically delete any racial slurs. Any command here requires the user to have the “manage server” permission.

Usage:

!modcommands
